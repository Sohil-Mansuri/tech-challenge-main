Acai Travel Cancellation and Refund Policy

Standard Cancellation Policy:
- More than 30 days before departure: Full refund
- 15 to 30 days before departure: 75% refund
- 7 to 14 days before departure: 50% refund
- Less than 7 days before departure: No refund

Flight Cancellations:
All flight cancellations are subject to airline policies.
Acai Travel will assist but cannot guarantee refunds for flights.

How to Cancel:
Contact us at support@acai-travel.com or call +34 93 000 0000
Cancellation must be submitted in writing.

Travel Insurance:
We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance.
Acai Travel partners with SafeTrip Insurance for comprehensive coverage.
